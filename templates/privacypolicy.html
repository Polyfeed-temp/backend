Introduction

PolyFeed is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the PolyFeed Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Use

User Authentication

PolyFeed is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the PolyFeed Chrome extension.

Data Storage

We store users' interaction with moodle feedback they received. The highlights, notes, to-do lists, and responses from ChatGPT will be stored in a secure relational database. When a record is deleted by a user, it is marked as inactive in the database rather than being permanently removed.

Data Visualization

The data collected is used to generate visualisations on the PolyFeed dashboard, offering insights into interactions with feedback, tracking common strengths and weaknesses, and monitoring the progression of action plans based on received feedback.

User Logs

We collect logs of user activity to analyse behaviour and improve the functionality of the tool. This data helps us understand how users interact with PolyFeed, allowing us to enhance user experience and service quality.

Data Security

We employ robust security measures to protect the data we handle. These include using HTTPS protocols on our website and securing all data transmissions to prevent unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction.

Compliance and Ethics

PolyFeed is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of data protection and ethical conduct. We regularly review our compliance policies and engage with legal and data protection experts to ensure that we meet all regulatory requirements and ethical standards in our operations.

Access and Control

Users have control over their information stored in the polyfeed tool. You can view, edit, or delete your data via the PolyFeed dashboard and extension. Data marked as inactive is securely stored and is not used for any processing or visualisation.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any significant changes and indicate the date of the latest revision.
