What is PolyFeed?

PolyFeed,is a feedback management system that aims to support learning by allowing students to manage feedback received on multiple assignments and across different courses in a single platform. Specifically, the browser extension of PolyFeed enables students to highlight and label feedback, make notes, create to-do lists, seek further clarifications from ChatGPT, and provide feedback on the feedback received from teachers. In addition, PolyFeed provides a dashboard visualising the insights from students' interactions with feedback, allowing them to track their common strengths and weaknesses in addition to the progression of their action plans based on feedback.
